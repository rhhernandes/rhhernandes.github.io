About

Journalist specializing in technology and data, currently studying for a master's degree in Ethics of AI, Data, and Algorithms at the University of Cambridge (UK). Formerly an editor-at-large at Folha de S.Paulo.

Applies data skills to reporting and writes about technology, especially the connection between tech and society, focusing on artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, metaverse, and ethics.

Also has experience with technology project management, plus five years supporting the digital efforts of Folha de S.Paulo, understanding user behavior and performing audience metric analysis.

Responsible for the Privacy for Journalists project in Brazil.

Skills include Python (PyTorch for deep learning), SQL, Tableau, Google Data Studio, Google Analytics, Adobe Analytics, Chartbeat, CrowdTangle, data analysis, writing, editing, communication, and translation.
